WALLIX
⚠️ PROXY-BASED CONNECTION
Proxies secure connections, potentially allowing direct interaction between endpoint and resource.
Potentially vulnerable to endpoint-based threat compromise
⚠️ TRADITIONAL AUTHENTICATION
Relies primarily on external MFA providers, password rotation, and credential vaulting.
More complex to manage, not future-proofed for passwordless
✗ NO USER-SIDE PROTECTION
Primarily protects resources; no inherent user-side RBI-WAF.
Limited protection against sophisticated web-based threats
⚠️ STANDARD AUDIT LOGGING
Detailed session logs and keystroke capture but without inherent cryptographic non-repudiation.
Less robust for compliance and forensic requirements
⚠️ REQUIRES PLUGINS FOR FEATURES
Typically agentless but can require endpoint agents/plugins for certain advanced features.
Higher deployment complexity and management overhead
